About Kehuco

Kehuco (K) Limited is a telecommunications, engineering and power solutions and services provider, incorporated in Nairobi, Kenya in 1985, registered in Tanzania (1995), Uganda (2007) and Rwanda (2008).

Our Mission

At Kehuco Limited, we are committed to delivering innovative and sustainable solutions in electrical engineering and energy management. Our goal is to enhance the efficiency, safety, and reliability of power systems for our clients worldwide.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to meet the changing energy landscape.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of professionalism and transparency.
  • Sustainability: Pioneering eco-friendly and energy-efficient solutions.
  • Safety: Prioritizing safety in every aspect of our services.
